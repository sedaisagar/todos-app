Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for people who want to focus on doing — not managing a complicated app.

Instant Task Entry

Type your task and hit Add — or press Enter. No categories to set up, no forms to fill, just frictionless capture.

Popular

Due Date Reminders

Attach a due date to any task. A visual badge keeps overdue and upcoming items impossible to miss.

Smart Filtering

Instantly filter by All, Active, Completed, or tasks with due dates. Focus only on what's relevant right now.

Flexible Sorting

Sort tasks by added date, due date, or name — ascending or descending. Always find what you're looking for.

Inline Editing

Click the pencil icon on any task to edit it right in place. No popups, no modals — just click, type, done.

Free

Zero Setup Required

Open the app and start. No account, no subscription, no onboarding tour. Your tasks are there when you need them.

How it works

Three steps to a clearer head.

The whole app is designed around a single idea: getting tasks out of your head and onto a list, fast.

01

Write it down

Type your task into the input field and optionally pick a due date. Press ADD or hit Enter.

02

Focus with filters

Use the Filter and Sort controls to surface what needs attention — active tasks, due items, or everything at once.

03

Check it off

Tick the checkbox when done. Watch your completed count grow and feel the satisfaction of a cleared list.
